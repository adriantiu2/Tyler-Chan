EAST WEST Market 東西市場

EAST WEST Market 東西市場 is a documentary series about Chinese communities around the world, individuals who are breaking stereotypes through self-expression and a shared love for hip-hop. These stories explore what it means to be Chinese today, beyond borders.

The title comes from EAST WEST Supermarket, an Asian supermarket in New Jersey that served as a cultural meeting point. That place became a symbol for the project, where East and West mix.

The artists a part of the series are DJs, dancers, tattoo artists, rappers who challenge the traditional definitions of Chinese identity. Their work blends contemporary culture with honor to history and heritage.
