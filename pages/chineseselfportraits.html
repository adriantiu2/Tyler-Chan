Chinese Self Portraits

This project is an ongoing series of self portraiture in my young adulthood with the purpose of preserving special memories with elderly family, documenting the passing down of cultural heritage traditions, and paying respect to generations who sacrificed for an easier, brighter future for my generation and I.
